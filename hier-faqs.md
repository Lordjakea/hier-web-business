# Hier – Frequently Asked Questions

---

## For Business Users

### Getting Started

**What is Hier and how is it different from a traditional job board?**
Hier combines social-media-style job posting, an applicant tracking system (ATS), AI-powered candidate scoring, messaging, and employee onboarding into a single platform. Instead of candidates scrolling through text-heavy listings, they see visual job posts in a feed — similar to Instagram — and double-tap to apply. You get more engaged applicants rather than passive ones who bulk-apply everywhere.

**How do I create a business account?**
Go to dashboard.hierapp.co.uk and click "Start for Free". You'll create an account, verify your email, and then set up your company profile before posting your first role.

**Do I need to download an app to manage my hiring?**
No. The primary tool for businesses is the web dashboard at dashboard.hierapp.co.uk, which works in any browser. There is also a mobile app version of the business tools available if you prefer to manage things on your phone.

**How do I set up my company profile?**
From the dashboard, go to Settings → Profile. You can add your company name, company number, a bio, contact email and phone, your address, and upload a logo. This information appears on your public profile page that candidates see in the app.

### Posting Jobs and Content

**What types of posts can I create?**
There are three types: Job posts (a full role with salary, employment type, sector, and location), Content posts (social-style posts to showcase your culture, team, or news), and Gigs (short-term or one-off pieces of work with a set budget rather than a salary).

**What media can I use in a post?**
Each post has a visual hero — either a photo or a video. Videos can be up to 3 minutes long and are cropped to a 4:5 portrait format (similar to an Instagram post). You can upload from your device or, on mobile, record directly in the app.

**Can I create a post as a draft before going live?**
Yes. When creating a job post, you can save it as a draft and come back to publish it later. Draft posts appear in your Jobs section filtered under "Draft".

**How do I archive or delete a post?**
From the Jobs page, each post has options to archive (hides it from candidates but keeps the data) or delete it permanently. Content posts can also be archived and restored.

**What is a Gig and how does it differ from a job post?**
A Gig is for short-term or freelance work. Instead of a salary, you set a budget for the piece of work. It's ideal for one-off projects like a logo design, a social media campaign, or a short-term contract.

**Can I add screening questions to my job post?**
Yes. When creating a job post, you can enable screening questions and add custom questions that candidates must answer as part of their application.

**What work modes can I set on a job post?**
You can set a job as Office, Hybrid, or Remote. This filters correctly in candidate searches.

### Managing Applicants

**Where do I see my applicants?**
The Candidates section of the dashboard shows all applications in a Kanban-style board. Each column represents a stage in the recruitment process.

**What are the recruitment stages in Hier?**
Applications move through: Applied → Shortlisted → Interview Offered → Interview Booked → Offered → Started. You can also mark an application as Rejected or a candidate can Withdraw themselves.

**Can I move candidates between stages in bulk?**
Yes. You can select multiple candidates and bulk-move them to a new stage, bulk-reject them, or bulk-archive them.

**Can I filter and search my applicants?**
Yes. You can filter by job post, stage, whether you've viewed the application, whether a rating or tags have been added, whether the CV has been viewed, and whether the application is from an archived post. You can also sort by newest or oldest.

**Can I add internal notes on a candidate?**
Yes. Inside each application you can add internal notes that are only visible to your team — candidates cannot see them.

**Can I rate candidates?**
Yes. You can give each applicant a star rating to help you prioritise and compare candidates for a role. The rating is for internal use only.

**Can I add tags to applicants?**
Yes. Recruiter tags let you label candidates with custom keywords to help organise your pipeline (for example, "strong culture fit" or "needs visa").

**Can I view a candidate's CV?**
Yes. Where a candidate has uploaded a CV, you can view it directly in the dashboard. The system tracks whether a CV has been viewed and when.

**How does interview scheduling work?**
Businesses can offer interview slots directly through the platform (available as an add-on or on higher plans). Candidates receive a notification and can book a slot. Confirmed interviews appear in the candidate's app.

**Can multiple recruiters use the same account?**
Yes. You can invite team members from the Team section of the dashboard. The number of recruiter seats depends on your plan.

### Hier Intelligence (AI)

**What is Hier Intelligence (HI)?**
Hier Intelligence is Hier's AI layer that analyses each application against your job post and gives the candidate a score. It is designed to help you prioritise who to review first rather than replacing your judgement.

**What is an HI Score?**
The HI Score is a 0–100 AI-generated relevance score for each applicant. Scores are grouped into four bands: Strong (78+, shown in green), Review (58–77, shown in blue), Maybe (38–57, shown in amber), and Low (below 38, shown in red).

**What does the AI base the score on?**
The AI assesses each application against the requirements of your specific job post — things like the candidate's experience, skills, headline, and CV content matched against the role's sector, employment type, experience level, and description.

**Does Hier AI write a summary for each applicant?**
Yes, with Hier AI or Hier AI Pro enabled, the system generates a short summary for each applicant covering their strengths, any flags worth noting, a fit label, and a recommended next action.

**Can Hier AI shortlist candidates for me automatically?**
Yes. You can trigger an AI shortlist from within a job post view. You can set the shortlist mode — Broad, Balanced, or Strict — depending on how selective you want the AI to be when suggesting candidates to move forward.

**Does AI replace my hiring decisions?**
No. The AI surfaces suggestions and scores to make your workflow faster. All decisions — moving stages, sending offers, rejecting applicants — remain with you and your team.

### Candidate Library

**What is the Candidate Library?**
The Candidate Library lets you search, score, and revisit previous applicants across all your roles. Instead of starting from scratch each time a similar vacancy opens, you can find and re-engage candidates who've applied before and may be a good fit.

**How do I use the Candidate Library?**
From the dashboard, go to Candidate Library. You can search by name, filter by role, and sort by HI Score, whether the candidate is actively looking, most recent application, or alphabetically. You can also bulk-shortlist candidates from the library directly into a new role.

**Which plan gives access to the Candidate Library?**
The Candidate Library is included in the Pro and Hier plans. It is also available as a standalone add-on at £99.99/month on other plans.

### Messaging

**Can I message candidates directly in Hier?**
Yes. The Messages section of the dashboard gives you an inbox to communicate with candidates. You can start a conversation directly from a candidate's application card.

**What plan do I need for messaging?**
Messaging is included in Pro and Hier plans. It is available as a standalone add-on (£99.99/month) on Starter and Growth plans.

**Can candidates message me first?**
Yes. Candidates can initiate conversations through the app, which will appear in your dashboard inbox.

### Analytics

**What analytics does Hier provide?**
The Analytics section shows you recruitment performance metrics including total views, applications, messages, and saves across your posts. You can also see conversion rates through your hiring funnel, which roles are performing best, and period-on-period trend comparisons. You can view data over 7, 30, 90, or 365 days, and filter by individual recruiter.

**Which plan includes analytics?**
Analytics is included in Hier plans. It is also available as a standalone add-on at £74.99/month.

### Boosting Posts

**What is a Boost?**
Boosting a post promotes it more prominently in the candidate feed, increasing the number of people who see it. Each boost uses credits and lasts a set number of hours depending on how many credits you spend.

**How do I get boost credits?**
Some plans include monthly boost credits. You can also purchase additional boost credit packs from the Promote section of the dashboard.

**Can I boost any post type?**
You can boost active job posts, content posts, and gigs.

### Onboarding and Employee Records

**Does Hier include an onboarding tool?**
Yes. Once a candidate has been offered a role and is at the "Started" stage, you can launch an onboarding journey for them directly from the dashboard.

**What can I include in an onboarding journey?**
An onboarding journey can include tasks (things the new starter needs to complete), documents (e.g. handbooks, right to work forms), and contracts (which can be sent and signed digitally via DocuSign). Each item tracks its status — sent, viewed, submitted, approved, waived, and so on.

**Can the new employee see their onboarding in the app?**
Yes. Candidates who have been moved to "Started" and have an onboarding journey in progress can see their tasks, documents, and contract status in the Hier mobile app.

**What is the Employee Records section?**
Employee Records is an HR management system built into Hier. Once someone has started, you can store their personal details (legal name, contact info, address, emergency contacts, start date, employee number, job title, department, manager, and more) all in one place alongside their onboarding journey.

**Which plan includes onboarding and employee management?**
These features are included in the Hier plan. They are also available as an add-on (£199.99/month) on other plans.

**What is the Starter plan good for?**
The Starter plan is a good fit for businesses hiring occasionally who want to test the platform. It includes basic job posting and applicant management.

## For Candidate Users

### Getting Started

**What is Hier?**
Hier is a job search app that works more like a social media feed than a traditional job board. You scroll through visual job posts from employers, double-tap on roles that interest you to apply, and then track where you are in the recruitment process — all in one place.

**Is Hier free for candidates?**
Yes. Hier is completely free for candidates. There are no subscription fees, no premium tiers, and no charges at any point in the process.

**Where can I download the Hier app?**
Hier is available for free on the Apple App Store (search "Hier Jobs") and on Google Play. It is available on both iOS and Android.

**Can I also use Hier on a computer?**
The candidate experience is designed for the mobile app. The dashboard at dashboard.hierapp.co.uk is for business users. Download the app for the best experience.

**How do I sign up?**
Open the app, tap Sign Up, and enter your email address. You'll receive a verification email — confirm it, then set up your profile and preferences to get started.

---

### Your Profile

**What should I include in my profile?**
Your profile has a name, headline (a one-line summary of who you are professionally), a short bio (up to 140 characters), your work experience, and a Showcase section for images, videos, or files that display your work. You should also upload your CV.

**How do I upload my CV?**
Go to your Profile tab and tap the CV section. You can select a document from your phone and upload it. Employers you apply to will be able to request and view your CV through the platform.

**What is the Showcase section?**
The Showcase lets you attach images, videos, or files to your profile — for example, a portfolio piece, a project you're proud of, or a short video introduction. It gives employers more context beyond a CV.

**Can I set my job preferences?**
Yes. In your profile you can set your preferred sectors, locations, employment types (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.), whether you're open to remote work, and a minimum salary. These preferences help surface relevant roles in your feed.

**Can employers see my profile without me applying?**
Your profile is only visible to businesses whose applications you've submitted, or businesses you've connected with through messaging. You are in control of your visibility.

---

### Finding and Applying for Jobs

**How does the feed work?**
The home feed shows a mix of job posts and company content posts from businesses on Hier. You scroll through them vertically, much like a social media feed. Each card shows the job title, company, location, salary (if listed), and a visual image or video from the employer.

**How do I apply for a job?**
Double-tap the post in the feed. If the job has no screening questions, your application is sent immediately. If there are screening questions, you'll be prompted to answer them before confirming your application.

**What are screening questions?**
Some employers include specific questions alongside the standard application — for example, "How many years of experience do you have in X?" or "Are you eligible to work in the UK?". You'll need to answer these before your application is submitted.

**Can I filter the feed?**
Yes. Tap the search icon to open the search and filter panel. You can filter by keyword, location, minimum salary, and maximum salary. There are also quick-keyword tags for common searches like Remote, Sales, Marketing, Tech, and Admin.

**Can I save a job to look at later?**
Yes. You can save jobs from the feed so you can come back to them without having to scroll back through everything.

**What is Auto Apply?**
Auto Apply is a feature you can enable in your profile preferences. When switched on, Hier will automatically submit your profile to relevant roles that match your preferences, without you having to double-tap each one manually.

**Can I withdraw an application?**
Yes. From your Applications tab, tap the application and select Withdraw if you no longer want to be considered for the role.

---

### Tracking Your Applications

**How do I see the status of my applications?**
Go to the Applications tab in the app. You'll see a list of all the roles you've applied for and the current stage of each application. You can filter by: All, Active, Interview, Offered, and Closed.

**What are the application stages I might go through?**
The stages are: Applied, Shortlisted, Interview Offered, Interview Booked, Offered, Started. If you're not moving forward, your application may be marked as Rejected, or you can Withdraw it yourself.

**Will I be notified when my application moves to a new stage?**
Yes. You'll receive in-app notifications when the employer moves your application to a new stage — for example, when you're shortlisted or when an interview is offered.

**What does "Interview Offered" mean?**
It means the employer has offered you an interview through the platform. You should see interview slot options to book from within your application.

**Can I see upcoming interviews in the app?**
Yes. Your Applications tab shows upcoming booked interviews with the date, time, location or meeting link, and any notes from the employer.

---

### Messaging

**Can I message employers directly?**
Yes, if the employer has messaging enabled. A message icon will appear in your conversation list. You can also receive messages from employers directly to your inbox in the app.

**Will I see it clearly if an employer messages me?**
Yes. Messages appear in your Messages tab and you'll receive a notification so you don't miss anything.

---

### Onboarding

**What happens in Hier after I've accepted a job offer?**
If the employer uses Hier's onboarding tools, you'll receive an onboarding journey inside the app. This can include tasks for you to complete (like providing bank details or signing a contract), documents to read, and contracts to review and sign — all managed from the Onboarding section of the app.

**How do I sign a contract through Hier?**
If your employer sends a contract via the Hier onboarding flow, you'll receive a notification. The contract is handled through DocuSign and you can review and sign it from within the app or via email.

**What does it mean if my onboarding shows "Awaiting Employer"?**
It means you've completed your part of a task or document and the employer needs to review or approve it before the process moves on.

---

### Following Companies

**Can I follow a company on Hier?**
Yes. You can follow businesses on Hier. Following a company means their content posts and job posts appear in your feed, making it easier to stay connected with employers you're interested in.

**Why would I follow a company before applying?**
It lets you see their culture, team, and activity over time before deciding to apply. It's similar to following a brand on social media — you get to know them before committing.

### Account and Privacy

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to your Profile tab, open the menu, and select the option to delete your account. You'll need to type a confirmation phrase to prevent accidental deletion. Deletion is permanent and removes your data from the platform.

**What data does Hier hold about me?**
Hier holds your profile information, CV, application history, and any messages or showcase content you've uploaded. For full details, see the Privacy Policy at hierapp.co.uk/privacy-policy.

**Does my current employer see that I'm on Hier?**
No. Businesses only see your profile if you have applied to one of their roles or connected with them directly. Hier does not notify your current employer that you have an account or that you are looking for work.

**How do I change my email or password?**
Account settings including password changes can be managed from within the app. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen.

**I didn't receive my verification email — what do I do?**
Check your spam or junk folder. If it's not there, try resending from the login screen. If you're still having trouble, contact Hier support at hello@hierapp.co.uk or call 0333 090 6227.
